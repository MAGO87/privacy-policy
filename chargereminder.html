Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

ChargeReminder respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

Information Collection and Use

ChargeReminder does not collect, store, or share personal data.

The App does not require user registration and does not access:

  • Name or surname
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Photos or files
  • Location data

Permissions

The App may request system permissions strictly necessary for its core functionality, such as battery monitoring and notifications.

These permissions are used only locally on the device and are not used to collect personal data.

Notifications

ChargeReminder may send local notifications to remind users about battery charging status. Notifications are generated entirely on-device.

Third-Party Services

The App may use trusted third-party services provided by Google Play Services for app distribution, stability, and performance.

These services may collect anonymous technical information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected, no personal information is stored or transmitted.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
